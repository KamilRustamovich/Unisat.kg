UniSat is a STEM program for girls, on assembling and launching a nanosatellite

For the first time in Uzbekistan, we launch a program that will increase the interest of girls in cosmonautics! Hurry up to register, to gain knowledge and skills that will be important for your future career and studies.

Are you interested in becoming an engineer, scientist, designer, mathematician, IT specialist? Participation in the UniSat program will help you to enroll in the best universities of Uzbekistan and the world!

Best mentors, engineers and scientists will help you gain new knowledge and teamwork skills

4 months of online and offline workshops, practical work

20 best participants will visit a university in Almaty, for 10 days of practice on assembling a nanosatellite

Launching a nanosatellite that you will assemble into the stratosphere, on April 12, 2022!

Info for participants

We invite girls from all regions of Uzbekistan to take part in this unique program! If you only dreamed of space — your dreams can come true! We will assemble a real nanosatellite and launch it into the stratosphere. Join us!

Recruitment of 150 girls, 15 to 24 years, from all regions of Uzbekistan

4 months of workshops, selection of 20 best participants, based on the assignments results

A trip to a university in Almaty, meeting with participants and mentors from Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan, a workshop on assembling a nanosatellite

Work in laboratories and launch of the nanosatellite on April 12, 2022

Our Workflow

Designing, developing, assembling, testing

Planning

Within the UNEPG course girls will get not only Hard Skills, but also Soft Skills

Designing

Within the UNEPG course  girls will go through all the stages of developing a real space object

Developing

Within the UNEPG educational course, 2 UniSat nanosatellites will be developed for launch

Launch

Launch of nanosatellites will be carried out using a helium balloon into the stratosphere
