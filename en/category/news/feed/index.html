They call it a “nanosatellite”!

 

You will learn:

  • what is a nanosatellite;
  • types of satellites CubeSat and their capabilities;
  • mission and device of nanosat.

 

Speakers:

  • Nora Spektr is an entrepreneur, mentor with more than 27 experience in advertising, marketing, commercial aviation, tourism and logistics, as well as a popularizer of space in Uzbekistan;
  • Aziz Razum is a start-up mentor in various programs, an expert in the field of telecommunications, financial technology and e-commerce.

 

Mark on your calendar and don’t miss the webinar!

  • December 4, Saturday;
  • at 18:00;
  • on the ZOOM platform.
